Some Fact about Cats 

		The average cat sleeps 16 to 18 hours per day.
		
		Male cat is called a "Tom" and a female cat is called a "Queen".
		
		Cats can make more than 100 different vocal sounds. 
		
		Cats can run up to 30miles per hour. 
		
		A cat can jump approximately seven times their height 
		
		Cats cannot taste anything sweet
		
		A cat's sense of smell is 14 times greater then human 
		
		Cat greet one another by touching their noses together
